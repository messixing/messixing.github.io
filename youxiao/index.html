About CEAS

了解中教优效

中教优效测评技术有限公司是一家从事教育测评服务的专业机构，总部位于深圳，在北京、广州等地设有分支机构，致力于以世界领先的教育测评技术，丰富的教育管理和实践经验，为政府机构，教育部门，企事业单位和学生提供安全、有效、可信、公平的教育测评服务。

CEAS is a professional organization designing and delivering assessments services in education, with its headquarters in Beijing and several regional operation centers in Tianjin, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Wuhan etc.. With leading-edge assessment technologies and abundant experiences in operating and administering large-scale examinations, CEAS serves governmental agencies, educational institutions, businesses and millions of learners of every kind by providing secure, valid, reliable and fair assessment programs.
